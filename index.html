ABOUT ME

I design with intention—blending storytelling, strategy, and aesthetics to create work that resonates. Obsessive about details but always focused on the bigger picture, I thrive on pushing boundaries, refining ideas, and creating visuals that leave an impact.

I love running marathons and I beleive, design, much like marathons, is about endurance, strategy, and knowing when to sprint or pace yourself. Both demand persistence, adaptability, and a vision that goes beyond just crossing the finish line. Whether I’m shaping a brand identity or clocking miles on the road, I’m in it for the long run.
